Basketball & Physics: My Three-Point Journey

Basketball Sports Life

Everyone knows I love architecture and Minecraft, but when I'm not building virtual skyscrapers, I'm usually on the court. Basketball is basically physics in motion!

I've been practicing my three-point shot lately. It's all about the arc. If the arc is too flat, the rim is "smaller" for the ball. If it's high enough, the ball has a much better chance of dropping in. It's just like calculating the perfect slope for a roof in Minecraft!

Being 12 means I'm not the tallest guy on the court yet, but speed and strategy make up for it. Just like in Genshin, team composition (or team chemistry) is everything. You need a good balance of shooters, defenders, and playmakers.

See you on the court!
