Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

Welcome to James's Amazing Autobiography (amazingjames.ca). Your privacy is highly important to us. This Privacy Policy document outlines the types of personal information that is received and collected by amazingjames.ca and how it is used.

1. Log Files

Like many other Web sites, amazingjames.ca makes use of log files. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, type of browser, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track user's movement around the site, and gather demographic information. IP addresses and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

2. Cookies and Web Beacons

amazingjames.ca does use cookies to store information about visitors preferences, record user-specific information on which pages the user access or visit, customize Web page content based on visitors browser type or other information that the visitor sends via their browser.

3. Google AdSense & Third-Party Advertisement

We use Google AdSense and other third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website.

  • Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on amazingjames.ca.
  • Google's use of the advertising cookie enables it and its partners to serve ads to users based on their visit to amazingjames.ca and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting the Google Ad Settings page.

These third-party ad servers or ad networks use technology to send the advertisements and links that appear on amazingjames.ca directly to your browsers. They automatically receive your IP address when this occurs. Other technologies (such as cookies, JavaScript, or Web Beacons) may also be used by the third-party ad networks to measure the effectiveness of their advertisements and/or to personalize the advertising content that you see.

amazingjames.ca has no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers.

4. Opting Out

You can choose to disable or selectively turn off our cookies or third-party cookies in your browser settings. Alternatively, you can opt out of a third-party vendor's use of cookies for personalized advertising by visiting aboutads.info.

By using our website, you hereby consent to our privacy policy and agree to its terms.
